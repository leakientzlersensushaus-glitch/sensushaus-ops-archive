# Access Scope Index

Selected internal scope labels used in protected-environment routing, review, and event history.

## Common Scope Labels

- `ARCH_REVIEW_READ`
  Architecture review and environment comparison. Read-only review scope.

- `FACILITIES_ENV_ADMIN`
  Facilities environment administration for routine room-state and environmental control management.

- `COMPLIANCE_REVIEW`
  Compliance and audit review scope. Review-only access.

- `VENDOR_DIAGNOSTIC`
  External vendor diagnostic support. No direct incident-mode control.

- `TCAP_CONTINUITY_OVERSIGHT`
  Lead-investor continuity review scope used for designated routing, audit, and review-control purposes under the Infrastructure Assurance and Continuity Oversight Addendum.
